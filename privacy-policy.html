TapToType
Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 25, 2026

TapToType ("the App") is a Bluetooth keyboard utility that turns your Android phone into a wireless keyboard for your PC. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

TapToType does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App operates entirely offline using Bluetooth technology. No account, registration, or sign-in is required.

Keyboard Input

All keystrokes entered in the App are transmitted directly to your connected PC via the Bluetooth HID (Human Interface Device) protocol. Keystroke data is:

  • Never stored on your phone beyond the moment of transmission
  • Never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party
  • Never logged or recorded by the App

Bluetooth Data

The App accesses your device's Bluetooth hardware solely to discover and connect to your paired PC. The following Bluetooth-related data is stored locally on your phone only:

  • Saved device names (user-assigned nicknames)
  • Bluetooth MAC addresses of saved devices

This data is never transmitted externally or shared with any third party.

Permissions

TapToType requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • Bluetooth Connect / Advertise / Scan — Required to discover, pair with, and connect to your PC as a Bluetooth keyboard
  • Foreground Service (Connected Device) — Required to maintain the Bluetooth keyboard connection when the App is running in the background

No other permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, location, etc.) are requested or used.

Data Storage

The App stores only the following data locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences:

  • App preferences (theme mode, default typing mode, enter key behavior)
  • Saved Bluetooth device names and addresses

No data is stored remotely. Uninstalling the App removes all stored data.

Third-Party Services

TapToType does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. The App contains no ads and makes no network requests (other than the optional in-app contact form).

Children's Privacy

TapToType is not directed at children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the App collects no personal data from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
